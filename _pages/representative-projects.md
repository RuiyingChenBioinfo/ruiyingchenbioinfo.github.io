## GEI-NJ

GEI-NJ is a gene-expression-informed neighbor-joining framework for constructing SNV-informed relationship trees from single-cell and spatial transcriptomic sequencing profiles.

- **Role:** Developer and first author
- **Repository:** [GEI-NJ](https://github.com/RuiyingChenBioinfo/GEI-NJ)

## LineMut

LineMut is a computational framework for detecting endogenous somatic SNVs with CellMetaBin resolution from single-cell and spatial transcriptomic sequencing profiles and generating SNV-constrained lineage hypotheses.

- **Role:** Co-developer and first author
- **Repository:** [LineMut](https://github.com/RuiyingChenBioinfo/LineMut_v0.2.0)
